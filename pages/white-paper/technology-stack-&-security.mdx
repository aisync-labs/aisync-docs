## Technology Stack and Security

## Backend Technology
- **High-Performance Asynchronous Service Framework:** Combining Node.js with NestJS and TypeScript as the main development language, providing an efficient event-driven architecture and comprehensive type safety support, enhancing the robustness and maintainability of backend services.
- **Microservices Architecture:** Developing performance-critical modules using Rust and Go, employing gRPC and Protobuf for efficient binary communication between microservices, ensuring low latency and high throughput in data transfer.
- **Containerization and Automated Deployment:** Utilizing Docker for containerization and Kubernetes for automated deployment, supporting seamless service scaling and management, and implementing Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) processes.

## AI Training

- **Advanced Machine Learning Frameworks:** Utilizing Python as the primary development language, combined with PyTorch and TensorFlow frameworks to implement complex deep learning and reinforcement learning models. Introducing emerging neural network architectures such as Transformers and Capsule Networks to enhance the expressiveness and interpretability of models.
- **Distributed Computing Support:** Using modern distributed computing frameworks like Ray and Dask to support large-scale parallel training tasks, optimizing resource utilization and reducing training times.
- **Adaptive Algorithm Optimization:** Integrating evolutionary algorithms and Bayesian optimization techniques to dynamically adjust hyperparameters during the learning process, achieving optimal performance in multi-objective optimization scenarios.

## Security and Compliance

- **Risk Management with Multi-Signature and Hardware Wallets:** Implementing risk control measures combining multi-signature and hardware wallets.
- **Encrypted Storage and Fine-Grained Permission Control for CEX APIs:** Adopting encryption for storage and detailed access control for CEX APIs.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Aligning closely with international regulations to prevent money laundering and illegal financing.
- **Encryption and Access Control:** Integrating Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) and TLS/SSL protocols for encrypted transmission and storage of all sensitive data. Implementing role-based access control (RBAC) and attribute-based access control (ABAC) policies to ensure strict separation of data access permissions.
