# Feeder Program: Genesis Spark

By linking your Solana wallet, our system will continuously channel that wallet’s data—encompassing interaction logs, trading strategies, and portfolio standings—into our AI-driven scoring engine for sophisticated calibration and evaluation. Guided by the resulting score model, AISYNC will confer point-based rewards and determine subsequent upgrades to your account privileges. This mechanism enables the seamless vertical integration of highly accurate data into the Agent marketplace, thereby empowering more robust and capable AI-driven applications.
